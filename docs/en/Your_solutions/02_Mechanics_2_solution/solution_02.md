## 2. Harmonic Motion


#### **1. Identify Given Values**
Comparing the given equation $x(t) = 0.2 \cos(10\pi t)$ to the standard form:
$x(t) = A \cos(\omega t)$
* **Amplitude ($A$):** $0.2\text{ m}$
* **Angular Frequency ($\omega$):** $10\pi\text{ rad/s}$
* **Mass ($m$):** $10\text{ kg}$

#### **2. Calculate the Spring Constant ($k$)**
Using the relation $\omega^2 = \frac{k}{m}$:
$$k = m \cdot \omega^2$$
$$k = 10 \cdot (10\pi)^2 = 1000\pi^2\text{ N/m}$$
**$k \approx 9869.6\text{ N/m}$**

#### **3. Calculate Total Mechanical Energy ($E$)**
Using the energy formula $E = \frac{1}{2} k A^2$:
$$E = \frac{1}{2} (1000\pi^2) \cdot (0.2)^2$$
$$E = 500\pi^2 \cdot 0.04 = 20\pi^2\text{ J}$$
**$E \approx 197.4\text{ J}$**
